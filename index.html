I am a theoretical physicist and dedicated PhD student with a focus on Particle Physics. With my curiosity and passion for science, I am currently pursuing my doctorate at King's College London. Before joining King's for my doctorate, I have graduated with a Bachelor and Master of Science with distinction from RWTH Aachen University. My academic journey has also taken me to UC Berkeley, where I spent part of my Master's.

My research is focused on some of the outstanding puzzles in Particle Physics: While the jury is still out on some anomalies measured in disagreement with predictions from the well-tested Standard Model of Particle Physics, other major problems remain. Among the latter I am particularly interested in elucidating the nature of dark matter and dark energy, and addressing the Electroweak Hierarchy Problem.
